イトヨリ (Itoyori)

Golden Threadfin Bream

Scientific Classification

Order: Perciformes

Family: Nemipteridae

Scientific Name: Nemipterus virgatus

English Name: Golden Threadfin Bream

About Itoyori

Known for its vibrant golden-pink hue, Itoyori, or Golden Threadfin Bream, is a highly valued fish in Japanese cuisine. Its delicate flavor and tender flesh make it ideal for steaming, grilling, or preparing as sashimi.

Seasonality

Itoyori is most abundant and flavorful during the spring and early summer months.

Did You Know?

In traditional Japanese cuisine, Itoyori is often used for celebratory dishes due to its beautiful coloration, symbolizing good fortune and prosperity.
