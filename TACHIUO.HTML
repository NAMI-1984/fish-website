太刀魚 (Tachiuo)

Largehead Hairtail

Scientific Classification

Order: Perciformes

Family: Trichiuridae

Scientific Name: Trichiurus japonicus

English Name: Largehead Hairtail

About Tachiuo

Tachiuo, or Largehead Hairtail, is a sleek and shiny fish valued in Japanese cuisine for its mild, sweet flavor and delicate texture. It is often grilled, simmered, or fried, making it a versatile ingredient.

Seasonality

Tachiuo is best enjoyed during summer and autumn when it is at its freshest and most flavorful.

Did You Know?

The name "Tachiuo" translates to "sword fish" in Japanese, referencing its long, sword-like body and metallic sheen.

Fun Fact

Tachiuo is a deep-sea fish that can thrive at depths of over 200 meters, contributing to its unique texture and flavor profile.
