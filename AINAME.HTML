アイナメ (Ainame)

Fat Greenling

Scientific Classification

Order: Scorpaeniformes

Family: Hexagrammidae

Scientific Name: Hexagrammos otakii

English Name: Fat Greenling

About Ainame

Ainame, or Fat Greenling, is a popular fish in Japan for its tender flesh and mild flavor. It is often prepared grilled, steamed, or in hot pots, showcasing its versatility in Japanese cuisine.

Seasonality

Ainame is best enjoyed during the spring and autumn months when it is at its most flavorful.

Did You Know?

In Japan, Ainame is commonly fished near rocky coastal areas and is considered a prized catch for its culinary value.
