サバ (Saba)

Mackerel

Scientific Classification

Order: Perciformes

Family: Scombridae

Scientific Name: Scomber japonicus

English Name: Mackerel

About Saba

Saba, or Mackerel, is a staple in Japanese cuisine, appreciated for its rich umami flavor and high omega-3 content. It is commonly grilled, marinated, or prepared as sashimi.

Seasonality

Saba is best enjoyed during autumn and winter when it is most flavorful and rich in oils.

Did You Know?

Saba is a key ingredient in "Shime-saba," a dish where the fish is marinated in vinegar to enhance its flavor and texture.

Fun Fact

Mackerel is known for its speed and agility, often swimming in large schools to evade predators. This behavior contributes to its firm and muscular texture.
