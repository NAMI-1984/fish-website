アジ (Aji)

Horse Mackerel

Scientific Classification

Order: Perciformes

Family: Carangidae

Scientific Name: Trachurus japonicus

English Name: Horse Mackerel

About Aji

Aji, or Horse Mackerel, is a popular fish in Japanese cuisine known for its rich flavor and versatility. Often served as sashimi, grilled, or deep-fried, it is cherished for its firm texture and umami-packed taste.

Seasonality

Aji is best enjoyed during the summer months, when it is most abundant and flavorful.

Did You Know?

Aji is often used in traditional Japanese dishes like Aji no Tataki (minced horse mackerel) and Aji Fry (deep-fried horse mackerel).
