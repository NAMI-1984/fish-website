ヒラメ (Hirame)

Olive Flounder

Scientific Classification

Order: Pleuronectiformes

Family: Paralichthyidae

Scientific Name: Paralichthys olivaceus

English Name: Olive Flounder

About Hirame

Hirame, or Olive Flounder, is a premium fish in Japanese cuisine, prized for its delicate, mild flavor and firm texture. It is often served as sashimi or sushi, highlighting its pristine quality.

Seasonality

Hirame is best enjoyed during winter when its flavor and texture are at their peak.

Did You Know?

The name "Hirame" translates to "flatfish" in Japanese, reflecting its unique shape and bottom-dwelling lifestyle.

Fun Fact

Hirame can change its coloration to blend with the ocean floor, making it an expert at camouflage and a skilled predator.
