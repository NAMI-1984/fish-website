イワシ (Iwashi)

Sardine

Scientific Classification

Order: Clupeiformes

Family: Clupeidae

Scientific Name: Sardinops melanostictus

English Name: Sardine

About Iwashi

Iwashi, or Sardine, is a staple in Japanese cuisine, valued for its rich omega-3 content and strong umami flavor. Commonly prepared grilled, marinated, or as a topping for sushi, it is a versatile fish enjoyed in many traditional dishes.

Seasonality

Iwashi is at its best during the summer months when the fish is at its most fatty and flavorful.

Did You Know?

Sardines have been a vital part of Japanese culinary and fishing culture for centuries, often used in making fish-based sauces and dried snacks.
