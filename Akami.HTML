赤身 (Akami)

Blue Fin Red Tuna

About Akami

Akami refers to the leaner part of the Blue Fin Tuna. Known for its deep red color and rich, savory flavor, it is a popular choice in sashimi and sushi dishes. This cut is prized for its balance of taste and texture.

Seasonality

Best enjoyed from late winter to early spring when the tuna's flavor is at its peak.

Did You Know?

Blue Fin Tuna is considered one of the most sought-after fish in Japanese cuisine, often featured in high-end sushi restaurants.

Ratings

Rarity: ★★ - Relatively accessible

Knowledge: ★★★ - Well-known to sushi enthusiasts

Culinary Importance: ★★★ - Commonly featured in fine dining

Taste: ★★★★★ - Exceptional flavor
