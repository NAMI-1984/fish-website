キンキ (Kinki)

Broadbanded Thornyhead

Scientific Classification

Order: Scorpaeniformes

Family: Sebastidae

Scientific Name: Sebastolobus macrochir

English Name: Broadbanded Thornyhead

About Kinki

Kinki, also known as the Broadbanded Thornyhead, is a highly prized fish in Japanese cuisine, renowned for its rich, fatty meat and exquisite flavor. It is often prepared grilled or simmered to enhance its natural umami.

Seasonality

Kinki is best enjoyed in winter when its flavor and fat content are at their peak.

Did You Know?

The name "Kinki" reflects its reddish color, which resembles gold, symbolizing prosperity in Japanese culture.

Fun Fact

Kinki can live in deep, cold waters, often found at depths exceeding 200 meters. This environment contributes to its unique flavor and firm texture.
