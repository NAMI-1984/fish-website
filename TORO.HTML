大トロ (Ōtoro)

Fatty Tuna

Scientific Classification

Order: Perciformes

Family: Scombridae

Scientific Name: Thunnus thynnus

English Name: Fatty Tuna

About Ōtoro

Ōtoro, or Fatty Tuna, is the most luxurious part of the tuna belly. Renowned for its rich marbling and melt-in-your-mouth texture, it is a must-try for sushi and sashimi lovers.

Seasonality

Ōtoro is best enjoyed during winter, when the tuna's fat content is at its highest, providing an exceptionally creamy flavor.

Did You Know?

Ōtoro comes from the underside of the tuna's belly, making it a very limited and highly prized cut. Its rarity and exceptional taste contribute to its premium value in the culinary world.

Fun Fact

The name "Ōtoro" combines "ō" (large) and "toro" (fatty), signifying its rich marbling and luxurious quality.
