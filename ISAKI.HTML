イサキ (Isaki)

Grunt

Scientific Classification

Order: Perciformes

Family: Haemulidae

Scientific Name: Parapristipoma trilineatum

English Name: Grunt

About Isaki

Isaki, also known as Grunt, is a beloved fish in Japanese cuisine for its mild, tender flavor and delicate texture. It is often served as sashimi or grilled, highlighting its natural sweetness and umami.

Seasonality

Isaki is best enjoyed from late spring to early summer, when the fish reaches peak flavor and fat content.

Did You Know?

In Japan, Isaki is often referred to as the "summer fish" because of its abundance and premium quality during the warmer months.
