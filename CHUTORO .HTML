中トロ (Chūtoro)

Medium Fatty Tuna

Scientific Classification

Order: Perciformes

Family: Scombridae

Scientific Name: Thunnus thynnus

English Name: Medium Fatty Tuna

About Chūtoro

Chūtoro, or Medium Fatty Tuna, represents the perfect balance between the lean meat of akami and the rich marbling of otoro. Its melt-in-your-mouth texture and rich flavor make it a favorite in sushi and sashimi dishes.

Seasonality

Chūtoro is best enjoyed during winter when the tuna's fat content is at its peak, offering a richer and more luxurious flavor.

Did You Know?

Chūtoro is often considered the most balanced and versatile cut of tuna, appreciated by both novice and seasoned sushi enthusiasts.

The Chūtoro section is relatively small compared to the entire tuna, making it a highly sought-after cut. Its limited availability adds to its allure and value in high-end cuisine.

Fun Fact

The name "Chūtoro" combines "chū" (middle) and "toro" (fatty), indicating its position as a medium-fatty part of the tuna belly.
