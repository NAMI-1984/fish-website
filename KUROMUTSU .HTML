黒ムツ (Kuro-mutsu)

Blackthroat Seaperch

Scientific Classification

Order: Perciformes

Family: Acropomatidae

Scientific Name: Doederleinia berycoides

English Name: Blackthroat Seaperch

About Kuro-mutsu

Kuro-mutsu, or Blackthroat Seaperch, is a premium fish in Japanese cuisine, valued for its rich, fatty flesh and deep flavor. Often prepared grilled or simmered, it is a favorite in high-end dining.

Seasonality

Kuro-mutsu is best enjoyed during winter, when its fat content is at its peak, enhancing its luxurious taste.

Did You Know?

The name "Kuro-mutsu" reflects its dark, sleek appearance. It is often caught in deep waters, making it a prized catch among fishermen.

Fun Fact

Blackthroat Seaperch is known for its versatility in Japanese cuisine, from traditional simmered dishes to modern sashimi presentations, showcasing its unique flavor profile.
