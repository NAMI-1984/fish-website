サワラ (Sawara)

Spanish Mackerel

Scientific Classification

Order: Perciformes

Family: Scombridae

Scientific Name: Scomberomorus niphonius

English Name: Spanish Mackerel

About Sawara

Sawara, or Spanish Mackerel, is a beloved fish in Japanese cuisine, known for its delicate, buttery flavor and high fat content. It is often grilled or broiled to highlight its rich taste.

Seasonality

Sawara is best enjoyed during spring and autumn when the fish is at its most flavorful.

Did You Know?

Sawara is a migratory fish that travels long distances along the coasts of Japan, making it a seasonal delicacy in different regions.

Fun Fact

In traditional Japanese cooking, Sawara is often marinated in miso or soy-based sauces to enhance its natural umami.
