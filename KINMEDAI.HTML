金目鯛 (Kinmedai)

Splendid Alfonsino

Scientific Classification

Order: Beryciformes

Family: Berycidae

Scientific Name: Beryx splendens

English Name: Splendid Alfonsino

About Kinmedai

Kinmedai, or Splendid Alfonsino, is a luxurious fish in Japanese cuisine, valued for its tender, fatty flesh and subtly sweet flavor. It is often served as sashimi, grilled, or simmered to showcase its exquisite taste.

Seasonality

Kinmedai is most flavorful during the winter months when its fat content is highest.

Did You Know?

The name "Kinmedai" translates to "Golden Eye Snapper," referring to its striking golden eyes, which are a distinguishing feature of the species.

Fun Fact

Kinmedai is often caught in deep waters exceeding 200 meters, contributing to its unique texture and rich flavor profile. Its bright red coloration makes it a symbol of celebration in Japanese culture.
