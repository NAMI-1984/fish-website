ホウボウ (Houbou)

Sea Robin

Scientific Classification

Order: Scorpaeniformes

Family: Triglidae

Scientific Name: Chelidonichthys spinosus

English Name: Sea Robin

About Houbou

Houbou, or Sea Robin, is a unique fish in Japanese cuisine, known for its firm flesh and delicate flavor. Its vibrant red coloration and distinctive pectoral fins make it a visually stunning catch.

Seasonality

Houbou is best enjoyed during winter, when its flavor and texture are at their finest.

Did You Know?

The name "Houbou" is derived from its ability to "walk" on the seabed using its modified pectoral fins. This characteristic has fascinated fishermen and seafood enthusiasts alike.

Fun Fact

Houbou is often used in high-end Japanese restaurants for sashimi or hot pot dishes, showcasing its versatility and premium quality.
