カマス (Kamasu)

Barracuda

Scientific Classification

Order: Perciformes

Family: Sphyraenidae

Scientific Name: Sphyraena japonica

English Name: Barracuda

About Kamasu

Kamasu, or Barracuda, is a long, sleek fish prized in Japanese cuisine for its tender meat and unique flavor. It is often grilled or fried, and its versatility makes it a favorite in many traditional dishes.

Seasonality

Kamasu is best enjoyed during the autumn months when it is most flavorful and rich in oils.

Did You Know?

The Japanese name "Kamasu" is derived from its sharp, pointed teeth, which resemble the blade of a kama (sickle).
