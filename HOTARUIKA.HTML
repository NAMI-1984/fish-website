ホタルイカ (Hotaru Ika)

Firefly Squid

Scientific Classification

Order: Myopsida

Family: Enoploteuthidae

Scientific Name: Watasenia scintillans

English Name: Firefly Squid

About Hotaru Ika

Hotaru Ika, or Firefly Squid, is a small luminous squid known for its bioluminescent blue light. It is a seasonal delicacy in Japan, particularly appreciated for its unique flavor and soft texture.

Seasonality

Hotaru Ika is best enjoyed in spring, with its peak season from March to June, when it is harvested fresh from Toyama Bay.

Did You Know?

The glowing light of Hotaru Ika is used to attract prey in the deep sea and creates a mesmerizing display during spawning season, attracting visitors from around the world to Toyama Bay.

Fun Fact

Hotaru Ika is often served as sushi, sashimi, or boiled in soy sauce, showcasing its rich umami flavor. It is also celebrated in festivals dedicated to its bioluminescence.
