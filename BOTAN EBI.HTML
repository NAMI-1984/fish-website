ボタンエビ (Botan Ebi)

Spot Prawn

Scientific Classification

Order: Decapoda

Family: Pandalidae

Scientific Name: Pandalus platyceros

English Name: Spot Prawn

About Botan Ebi

Botan Ebi, or Spot Prawn, is a luxurious delicacy in Japanese cuisine. Known for its sweet and tender meat, it is often served raw as sashimi or lightly grilled to highlight its natural flavors.

Seasonality

Botan Ebi is most commonly enjoyed during spring and early summer, when it is at its freshest.

Did You Know?

Botan Ebi is prized for its vibrant red color and is considered a symbol of celebration in Japan, often featured in festive dishes.

Fun Fact

Despite its delicate appearance, the Spot Prawn is known for its hardy nature, thriving in cold, deep waters.
