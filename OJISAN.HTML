オジサン (Oji-san)

Goatfish

Scientific Classification

Order: Perciformes

Family: Mullidae

Scientific Name: Parupeneus multifasciatus

English Name: Goatfish

About Oji-san

Oji-san, or Goatfish, is a unique fish recognized for its vibrant colors and delicate flavor. It is often prepared grilled or steamed, making it a versatile choice in Japanese cuisine.

Seasonality

Oji-san is best enjoyed during the warmer months, especially in late spring and early summer when it is at its freshest.

Did You Know?

Goatfish are known for their distinctive whisker-like barbels, which they use to search for food on the ocean floor.
